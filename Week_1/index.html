Facts about Kateda

  • This martial art actually began to be developed from Indonesia since 1963 on Mount Bromo by Grand Master Takashi
  • The KATEDA symbol is taken from the mountain symbol.
  • On 5 March 1981, "London KATEDA School of Defense" became the center of all KATEDA schools

Technical Movements

  • Punch
  • Kick
  • Slam
  • Withstand blunt force attacks from any direction
